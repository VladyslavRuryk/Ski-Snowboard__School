Were not saying Snow Mountain has special powers, but a lot of magical things do happen here. The slopes on Bald and Dollar mountains enjoy a nearly lift linefree existence all winter long, not to mention a nearly cloud-free one.

To those who wish to experience the best holiday time ever, we are excited to offer unlimited fun on the slopes, homey cosiness, wonderful views and snow activities.
